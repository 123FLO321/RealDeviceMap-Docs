Accounts

.. include:: ../../formatting.rst

If you have accounts added, the top of this page will give you information on how they are being used or what condition they are in.

Add Accounts
------------
- :underline:`Account Level`: The level of the accounts to be imported.
- :underline:`Accounts`: A list of accounts to import. Can be in either the following formats::
    
    username1,password1
    username2,password2
    username3,password3

  ::

    username4 password4
    username5 password5
    username6 password6
